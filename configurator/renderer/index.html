Common causes on Wi-Fi networks:
  • The worker service may not be running on the other laptop
    Windows: open Services, check "LlamaRPCBeacon" is Running
    macOS: run: sudo launchctl list | grep rpccluster
    Linux: run: systemctl status rpc-cluster-beacon
  • Your router may block device-to-device UDP traffic
    Try connecting both laptops via Ethernet instead
  • Windows Firewall may be blocking UDP port 5005 on the worker
    Run on the worker: netsh advfirewall firewall add rule name="RPC Beacon" dir=in action=allow protocol=UDP localport=5005
  • The worker beacon may be sending to the wrong IP
    On the worker, run: node worker-beacon/beacon.js
    Look for: "Broadcast address: 192.168.x.255" — must match your subnet
